1. Introduction

Welcome to BillyAI ("we," "our," or "us"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Invoice Data: Information extracted from scanned or uploaded invoices, including IBAN, recipient name, payment reference (omschrijving), and payment value.
  • Camera and Images: Invoice images are processed locally and via Google Vision API. We do not store these images permanently.
  • Device Information: Non-personal data such as device type, operating system, and unique identifiers for app optimization.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Extract invoice details using OCR and AI-powered processing.
  • Display extracted data in an easy-to-copy format.
  • Enhance user experience through anonymized analytics.
  • Ensure app functionality and performance optimization.

4. Use of Cloud Services and Third-Party APIs

  • Google Vision API: Converts images into readable text.
  • OpenAI API: Analyzes OCR text for structured invoice details.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your invoice details are securely stored in Firebase Firestore, linked to an anonymous identifier.

Images captured using the camera feature are not stored after processing.

6. Permissions Requested by the App

  • Storage Access (Temporary): Used for handling invoice images during processing.
